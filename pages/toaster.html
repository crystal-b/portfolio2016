Toaster Oven Interface

Redesign of a toaster oven interface

Brief
This is an unsolicited redesign of a poorly-rated toaster oven. I researched the reviews to figure out what users did and did not like about their experience with the toaster oven. I broke down their desires into a variety of categories including reliability, unobtrusiveness, clarity and contemporary design. I also listed their needs as necessary functions including toasting, baking, and reheating. Then I created personas to figure out what was most important for the interface.
