LOST Items
Data Visualization | JSON | JavaScript

For this data visualization for my Data Art class, I used P5.js to remix Will Field's visualization of items lost in the MTA. The assignment focused on aesthetics rather than accurate representation so the most frequently lost items are darker and appear the most concrete while items lost the least disappear into the light.
